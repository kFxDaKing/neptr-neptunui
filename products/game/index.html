FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions and we have answers. Below is a compiled list of commonly asked questions for your assistance.

How do I access my purchased instances?

First, simply ensure you've created an account on our control panel using the "Create An Account" email that was sent following your purchase. Then, access our control panel here.

How long does it take for my server to be deployed?

Server deployment is automatic, after we've received your order our systems will automatically authorize and generate your server. Refer to your emails for information once the installation has complete.

How do I receive a refund?

If you're not satisfied with a product, you can return it within a week from the purchase to receive a full refund. This can be requested in our billing panel by clicking "request cancelation".

How can I get in contact with the support team?

If you need assistance with technical support-related inquiries, you can contact our Help Department here. For sales-related issues, you can contact the Sales Department via the live-chat or here.
